# Support

Thank you for using our project. If you need support, please follow the guidelines below.

## How to get support

1. **Check the documentation**: Before reaching out, please check our [documentation](link-to-documentation) for answers to common questions.
2. **Search the issues**: Look through the [issue tracker](link-to-issues) to see if your question has already been answered.
3. **Open a new issue**: If you can't find an answer, please open a new issue in the [issue tracker](link-to-issues). Provide as much detail as possible to help us understand and resolve your issue quickly.

## Contact us

For additional support, you can reach out to us via:

- **Email**: support@devanox.com

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.

Thank you for your support!
